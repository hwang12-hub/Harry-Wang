About HaoYu Equestrian Club

A lifestyle of grace, balance, and partnership

At HaoYu Equestrian Club, we believe that equestrianism is more than a sport ‚Äî it is a lifestyle of grace, balance, and partnership. We are committed to providing the highest quality equestrian experience for every member, whether pursuing leisure riding or competitive performance.

HaoYu Equestrian Club was established in 2010. After more than a decade of development, it has become one of the leading equestrian clubs in China. We boast world-class facilities, a professional coaching team, and carefully selected premium horses.

We value refinement, discipline, and connection with the horse. We believe that by establishing a deep connection with horses, riders can not only improve their equestrian skills but also cultivate patience, responsibility, and an elegant attitude toward life.

"Step into a living tapestry where the soft echo of hooves on ancient earth mingles with the hush of silk skirts brushing polished leather. Here, every dawn begins with the scent of warm bay rum and sun-dried cedar, as riders in impeccably tailored coats greet mounts whose bloodlines stretch back farther than the family crests embroidered on their saddle pads."
Join a community that celebrates elegance, tradition, and the timeless bond between horse and rider.
